How it’s made?

  • Ice cream is a product obtained by freezing and whipping a mixture of natural milk, cream, condensed or dry whole and skimmed milk, various flavoring and aromatic substances and a stabilizer. Good taste ice creams are complemented by its high nutritional value. The components of ice cream in the production process are practically not change, so it is absorbed by (95...98)%. Milk-based ice cream, containing milk fat - up to 15%, proteins (3-6)%, sugars from 14 to 32%, minerals, vitamins A, groups B, D, K. Fruit and berry ice cream and ice cream with fruit and berry fillings are rich in vitamin C.
